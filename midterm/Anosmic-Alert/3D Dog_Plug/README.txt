# Summary

Outlet covers for dog people. To be honest, I don't really like the design my self, but I had made the PigPlug cover (http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1302145), and even though pig are somewhat cool, dogs are just way cooler. Therefore I had to make this design also. 

I have scaled Tioh's design to the size of the nose of my dog, Darwin, whom I could not get to sit still for the photo session.
